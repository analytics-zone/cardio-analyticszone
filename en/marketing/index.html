Short Description

Monitor 11 cardiovascular and mobility metrics with complete privacy. Evidence-based alerts, medication tracking, and professional reports for your clinician. All data stays on your device.

Key Features

  • 11 Comprehensive Metrics - Heart rate, blood pressure, HRV, SpO‚ÇÇ, ECG, VO‚ÇÇ Max, walking speed, and more

  • Privacy by Design - 100% on-device processing. No cloud, no accounts, no tracking
  • Professional Reports - Export PDF and CSV reports for your healthcare provider
  • HealthKit Integration - Seamless sync with Apple Health, background updates, write-back support

Value Propositions

Complete Cardiovascular Visibility

Track all your heart health metrics in one unified dashboard. From heart rate and blood pressure to ECG and walking speed - see the complete picture of your cardiovascular health.

Evidence-Based Insights

All metrics backed by clinical guidelines from Mayo Clinic, American Heart Association, Cleveland Clinic, and peer-reviewed research from NEJM, JAMA, and JACC. Know when your readings are concerning.

Your Privacy, Guaranteed

100% on-device processing means your health data never leaves your iPhone. No cloud servers, no accounts, no tracking. You control what to share and when.

Empower Clinical Conversations

Export professional reports with all your cardiovascular trends, medications, symptoms, and correlations. Give your doctor the data they need for better-informed decisions.
