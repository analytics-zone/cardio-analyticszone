Cardio Analytics Features

Complete cardiovascular health tracking with personalized dashboard, medication adherence, evidence-based alerts, and privacy by design

Personalized Dashboard

Track all 11 cardiovascular and mobility metrics in one unified view. See trends, compare against guideline ranges, and monitor changes at a glance.

  • Resting and walking heart rate - Monitor cardiovascular status with normal range indicators (60-100 bpm)
  • Blood pressure tracking - AHA category bands (Normal <120/80, Elevated, Stage 1, Stage 2)
  • Heart rate variability (HRV) - SDNN and RMSSD metrics for stress and health assessment
  • Oxygen saturation (SpO‚ÇÇ) - Track respiratory health with hypoxemia alerts (<90%)

Medication Adherence & Correlations

Track medication doses and visualize how they relate to your cardiovascular metrics.

  • Manual dose logging - Record medication intake with timestamps
  • HealthKit Medications sync - Automatically import medication records from Apple Health
  • Adherence rate calculation - Track how consistently you take prescribed medications
  • Correlation visualizations - See how medications affect your BP, heart rate, HRV, and weight
  • Trend analysis - Identify patterns between medication adherence and health outcomes

Privacy by Design

Your cardiovascular data stays on your device. You control what to share and with whom.

  • Granular HealthKit authorization - Choose exactly which data types to share
  • 100% local storage - All data processed and stored on your iPhone
  • No cloud servers - No external data transfers, no accounts required
  • No tracking or analytics - We don't collect usage data or personal information
  • You decide what to share - Export reports only when you choose

How HealthKit Sync Works

Seamless integration with Apple Health for effortless cardiovascular monitoring.

  • First-party HealthKit identifiers - Uses official Apple data types for all 11 metrics
  • Background updates - HKAnchoredObjectQuery with background delivery for fresh data
  • Battery-efficient - Delta sync means no battery drain from constant polling
  • Write-back support - User entries (weight, BP) can be written to HealthKit for consistency
  • Compatible with all devices - Works with Apple Watch, fitness trackers, manual entries, and connected devices
